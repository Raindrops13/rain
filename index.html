ways to reduce your environmental impact

Our enviromnent is so much more resiliant than what many of us believe. However, like literally any other living thing in the entire universe, if it does not get the time it needs to revive itself but instead is continously broken down and stripped of its resources, it will eventually die. So to oppose this fate, us humans must fufill our duty to the planet.

  • use less plastic: from straws and cups to even bags, plastic can be detremental to our natural bioshere
  • eat less fish
  • reuse containers and other 1 use items
  • volunteer to clean polluted areas
  • plant trees
  • be aware of how much of something you use
  • join public awareness opportunities
  • use renewable energy resources
